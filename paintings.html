Oil Paintings

My first ever oil painting was of a squash, a lemon, and a wooden ladle. I was 11. On Friday afternoons, I'd paint with a family friend while her napping Great Dane let out unbelievably smelly farts. Sue taught me to paint what I see, not what I think I see. In retrospect, it was an idyllic, and my first real introduction to art.
