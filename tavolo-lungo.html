Tavolo Lungo

Italian for 'long table'—Tavolo Lungo is a restaurant that embraces the timeless joy of feasting together. While the place, time, and food may change, the people gathered around the table stay the same. It's these eccentric characters that bring Tavolo Lungo to life.
